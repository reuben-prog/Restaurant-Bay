Our Menu

We offer both a refined brunch service and an elegant dinner service. Our cuisine is modern Australian, with a deep focus on showcasing the finest seasonal and Tasmanian produce.

Our menus are guided by the seasons and the close relationships we hold with our local farmers and purveyors. We invite you to explore our current offerings.
